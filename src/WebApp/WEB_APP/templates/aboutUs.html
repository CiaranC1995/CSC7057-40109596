About Us

Bot or Not is a text classifier aimimg to distinguish between text written by a human and text generated by AI.

This is especially important today with the fast genesis of large language models capable of outputting text that is to the naked eye indistinguishable from human-written text.

This classifier can be used by anyone interested in determining the authorship of a piece of text, no matter the domain or industry.

NOTE: As is the nature of machine learning, it is possible for this classifier to output incorrect results.. Therefore it is important that a classification result not be used as proof in plagiarism disputes or as a means to punish anyone in any professional or academic context.

Further expert analysis of text is needed to definitely prove the text authorship.

Features

Anyone can use this classifier as an anonymous user. However, an account is needed if you want to download your results from the application and/or keep a record of previous classifications.

This classifier is a free and open source university dissertation project. Anyone interested in seeing the code and suggesting any improvements for future iterations can follow the GitHub link at the bottom of any page.

User feedback is an important aspect of our developement life cycle so please feel free to use the Contact Us form to leave a message for our team.
